About Us

Charleston Assisted Living

Charleston Assisted Living is a residential assisted living caring for up to 8 residents. Charleston provides care and services for residents in a home-like environment. Our trained staff utilizes special techniques necessary to properly care for and meet residents' needs. Such techniques are more successful in a small, soothing environment where there is less "hustle and bustle" for the residents. Residents receive individualized attention 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, from caregivers trained in an assisted living environment, which includes a registered nurse-administrator. The low resident to staff ratio allows our caregivers to build and strengthen caring bonds with our residents on a daily basis.

The Management Team

The management team shares an extensive medical experience that includes over 15 years of working with patients in hospice, hospitals, assisted and skilled nursing facilities in the Northern Colorado area.

  • Lisa Foster

    Lisa Foster, a registered nurse, serves as the Charleston Assisted Living Administrator. She is a resident of Erie, Colorado, where she is an active community member very familiar with the area. Lisa’s nursing experience spans 15 years with the geriatric population, and she serves as an advocate for all her residents. Lisa’s previous nursing experience includes working in Assisted Living, Skilled Nursing and Hospice in Northern Colorado.

Location

Located in the Town of Erie, Colorado.

The reasons people call Colorado home often include a passion for the outdoors, a diverse and dynamic community and the contemporary pace to life that appeals to all ages. As you will soon discover, the Town of Erie offers the best of all of these. With our majestic mountain views and progressive civic vision, we provide a genuine small-town feel with all the reassuring qualities that implies – a sense of community, neighbors who care, and a contemporary town with modern amenities and comforting closeness.

Growing families, young professionals, outdoor enthusiasts, and active adults are drawn to our panoramic location, scenic trails, championship golf course, recreational activities, and more than 300 days of sunshine per year. Of course, many are also attracted by our premier educational facilities, which include new and planned schools.

Factor in our award-winning 63,000 square foot Erie Community Center and 20,000 square foot Erie Community Library; and our quality of life just keeps getting better and better.
